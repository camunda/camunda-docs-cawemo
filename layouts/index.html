Cawemo

Cawemo is the specification platform of the Camunda stack. Its main purpose is to enable all stakeholders to model and collaborate on BPMN and DMN diagrams and related files.

Cawemo comes in two flavours: the SaaS version hosted by Camunda and the on-premises version for self-hosting. For using the SaaS version, please go to https://cawemo.com.
